4.8 Curtain Wall 
The following objects will be modelled for curtain wall plan:  
  Embed 
  Mullion and transom 
  Glass panel and louvre

85  
 
 
4.8.1 Create Embed 
In Ribbon, click “Structure” ➜ click “Component” under “Model” 
 
Select the specific item in properties panel ➜Place it to the designed location 
 
 
4.8.2 Create mullion and transom 
Create Curtain wall by clicking “Architecture” ➜ “Wall” ➜ select “Curtain Wall” in 
the properties panel 
 
Create curtain grid by clicking “Architecture” ➜ “Build” ➜“Curtain Grid”

specific type of mullion in properties ➜ Place it to the designed curtain grid
