1 Hardware / System Requirements 
 
1.1 General 
 
Hardware / system requirement for modellings, coordination and 
visualisation on desktop/notebook computers and mobile devices 
should be determined by the BIM managers for different projects on 
a case by case basis. The minimum requirement varies for different 
applications, project sizes and operating systems.
