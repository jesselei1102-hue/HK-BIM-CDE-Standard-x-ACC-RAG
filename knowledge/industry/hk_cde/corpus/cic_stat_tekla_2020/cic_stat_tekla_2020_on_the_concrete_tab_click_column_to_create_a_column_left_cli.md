4.2 Creating Columns 
 On the Concrete tab, click Column to create a column. Left-click in 3D model space to create a 
column. In the property menu, assign a name for the column; simply change the numbers in the 
profile entry in property menu (such as 250*675 or 400*600) to adjust column size; type D -
diameter length (such as D1800) to change it to a circular column. Press three dots at the right for 
specific section profile. Change values in Top and Bottom to adjust column elevation. Right -click 
the column and choose move special > linear to fine adjust the position of the column in 3D space.
