4.3 Creating Panels ( Walls ) 
 On the concrete tab, click Panel > Panel to create walls. Left-click to define wall alignment on the 
plan view, press middle mouse button to finish. In the property menu, assign a name for the wall, 
Wall height and wall thickness can be adjusted in the profile entry in the property menu.
