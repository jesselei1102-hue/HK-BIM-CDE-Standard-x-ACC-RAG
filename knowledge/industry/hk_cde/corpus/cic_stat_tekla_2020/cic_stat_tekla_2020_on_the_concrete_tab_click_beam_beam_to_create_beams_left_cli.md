4.4 Creating Beams 
On the Concrete tab, click Beam > beam to create beams. Left-click in 3D model space to define 
endpoints of the beam. In the property menu, assign a name for the beams; like columns, simply 
change the numbers in the profile entry in property menu (such as 400*600 or 200*300) to adjust 
beam size; press three dots at the right for specific section profile. Right-click the beams and 
choose move special > linear to fine adjust the position of the beams in 3D space. 
 
  
 
For specially shaped beams such as haunch beam, stepped beams and cranked beams, it is 
suggested to use polygon cut to remove unwanted portion of the beam.
