# 4.5 Creating Slab Openings

13 
 
4.5 Creating Slab Openings  
Left-click the slab which needs to create slab opening, on the concrete tab, Press “ctrl+P” to get a 
top view in Tekla modelling space, choose polygon cut on the concrete tab to create slab opening. 
4.6 Creating a Staircase 
Click the application & components tab the right, search stair and chose concrete stair (component 
number 65) to create stair. Double-click the blue “M” symbol to adjust the properties of a 
component, click Modify > Apply > Okay to finish. Click the green buttons at the selection switches  
at the bottom to enable/disable user’s ability to choose parts inside components. Delete unwanted 
components if necessary. Deleted parts in the component will be regenerated whenever users 
click Apply > Okay in the component property menu.
