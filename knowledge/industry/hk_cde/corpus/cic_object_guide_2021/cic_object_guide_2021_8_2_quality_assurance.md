CIC PRODUCTION OF BIM OBJECT GUIDE

8.2 Quality Assurance 
The BIM object shall be checked by using a comprehensive check form and shall be endorsed  by the inspector to verify the 
BIM object functions and behaviour. This quality assurance check form should also be provided by BIM object creators for self-
checking, and to the client or receiver for inspection. 
The check form shall include the following items:
