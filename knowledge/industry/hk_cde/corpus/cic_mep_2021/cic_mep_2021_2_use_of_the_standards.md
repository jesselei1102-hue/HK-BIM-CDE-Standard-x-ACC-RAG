2 Use of the Standards 
This section describes the recommended approach in defining the required LOIN for MEP 
in a project, the approach is broken down into a number of steps in the table below, along 
with the responsible parties and reference to relevant sections of this or other documents. 
With this arrangement, the Appointing Party / Client can define their requirements step-by-
step and use this  Standards as guidance to the relevant LO IN requirements and other 
details. The LOIN will form a key part of the BEP.
