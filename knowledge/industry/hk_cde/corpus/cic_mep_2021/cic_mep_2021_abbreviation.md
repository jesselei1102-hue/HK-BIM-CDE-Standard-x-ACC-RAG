The CIC BIM Standards for Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP)  requirements are 
expressed in sentences in which t he principal auxiliary verb is “shall”. Recommendations 
are expressed in sentences in which the principal auxiliary verb is “should”. The use of the 
auxiliary verb “can” indicates that something is technically possible and the auxiliary verb 
“may” indicates permission.
