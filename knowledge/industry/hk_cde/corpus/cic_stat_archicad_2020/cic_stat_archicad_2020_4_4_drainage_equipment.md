# 4.4 Drainage Equipment

34 
 
 4.4 Drainage Equipment  
In the toolbar, click Equipment Tool  in Toolbar. Select relative Library.  
 
Choose the item on the left and edit it on the right if you want.   
 
 
If you want to create your drainage equipment, please refer to the following guide as a 
reference.
