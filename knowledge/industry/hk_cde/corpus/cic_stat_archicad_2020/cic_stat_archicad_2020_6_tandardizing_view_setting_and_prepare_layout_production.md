6. tandardizing View Setting and Prepare Layout Production 
6.1 Saving view 
After finishing the drainage modelling, the user can save specific floor plan or 3D 
perspective view in view map for drainage submission 
Click Project Chooser and choose Navigator.  
 
 
Click the view map and click save current view.  
 
 
 
In the save view dialogue, set the general setting and click create.
