2.16. Spatial Coordination Process 
Navisworks Manage will be used to check clash of visible E&M services. For any issue found in 
the clash analysis process, our BIM Engineer will attend BIM meetings to solve the potential 
problems with client before submitting as-built BIM model for approval and proceeding with the 
transition to the EOMS system.
