# 1.2 Parties

1 Information Management – [aligned with ISO 19650] 
 
 
28 
 
1.2 Parties 
 
As illustrated in Figure 7, a n essential aspect of effective information management are 
clear definitions of ownership,  responsibility and accountability  among the following parties 
and project functions:  
 
• Appointing Party / Client (Employer) 
o Appointing Party / Client  delivery management function;  
o Appointing Party / Client information management function;  
o Asset information management function; and 
o Security information management functions . 
 
• Lead Appointed Party (Lead Consultant / Lead Contractor) 
o Project delivery management; and 
o Project information management. 
 
• Appointed Parties (Task Team management functions) 
o Task management ; 
o Task information management ; 
o Interface management ; and 
o Information authoring.
