LA.3.8 Sub-Field 7: Sequential Number 
(Optional) A sequential number should be assigned when it is necessary to further distinguish 
the model from the others. It can also be used to distinguish other documents such as drawings. 
 
The numbering for standard coding should be fixed within the project information standard and it 
is suggested that the code for the number field be a maximum of three numbers in length. 
 
Note: Leading zeros should be used and care should be taken not to embody information that is 
present in other fields.
