# 1.3 Information Management Process

1 Information Management – [aligned with ISO 19650] 
 
33 
 
This function is likely to be undertaken by all those working within a Task Team. 
 
 
1.3 Information Management Process 
The Information Management Assignment Matrix shown in Appendix A Information 
Management Assignment Matrix Example, identifies each of the information management 
functions mapped to Information Tasks. This provides clear ownership and responsibility 
against each function.  
 
Examples of how this can be applied to projects are found in Section  3.
