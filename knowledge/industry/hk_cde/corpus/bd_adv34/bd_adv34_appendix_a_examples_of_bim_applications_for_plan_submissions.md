General Building Plans  innovative building design, 
irregular/twisted building form; 
 projecting features on external 
wall; 
 relationship between site 
profiles/street levels and proposed 
building;  
 area demarcations and calculations; 
 arrangement of means of escape 
and compartmentation;  
 compatibility between different 
types of plans; 
 spatial arrangement of building; 
 relationship between existing

and addition (A&A) works. 
 sequence and phasing 
of various stages2 of 
new building 
development; 
 sequence and phasing 
of A&A works. 
Drainage Plans   complex drainage systems and/or 
connections; 
 relationship between proposed 
underground drainage works and 
foundation works/site formation 
works, etc;  
 compatibility between different 
types of plans.  
 sequence and phasing 
of various stages2 of 
new building 
development; 
 sequence and phasing 
of A&A works. 
Superstructure Plans  material and grade of different 
structural elements; 
 fire resistance rating and concrete 
cover of different structural 
elements; 
 complex steel structures and/or 
connections; 
 sequence and phasing 
of various stages2 of 
new building 
development; 
 sequence and phasing 
of A&A works.
