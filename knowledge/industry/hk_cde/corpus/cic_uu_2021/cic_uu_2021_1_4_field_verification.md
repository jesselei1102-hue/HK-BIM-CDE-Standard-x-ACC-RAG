1.4 Field Verification  
Field verification of the model elements is important for most projects in Hong Kong. In 
most local and international BIM standards, “fiel d verified” is the main criterion for the 
definition of an LOD 500 element s. However, in terms of geometry, a model elements  
cannot be more detailed tha n those required for  fabrication (LOD-G 400). Therefore the 
criterion for “field verification” of a model elements should preferably be extracted from the 
LOD, e.g. a model elements with LOD-G 300 can also be field verified.  
 
The field verification techniques commonly used for the MEP discipline may  not be 
appropriate for verification of UU. The verification of UU is usually conducted by 
conventional survey or 3D digital survey.
