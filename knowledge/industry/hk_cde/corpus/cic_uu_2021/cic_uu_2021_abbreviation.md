The CIC BIM  Standards for Underground Utilities  (UU) requirements are expressed in 
sentences in which the principal auxiliary verb is “shall”. Recommendations are expressed 
in sentences in which the principal auxiliary verb is “should”. The use of the auxiliary verb 
“can” indicates that something is technically possible and the auxiliary verb “may” indicates 
permission.
