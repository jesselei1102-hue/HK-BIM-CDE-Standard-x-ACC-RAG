3 Use of the Standards 
This section describes the recommended approach in defining the scope of BIM for UU in 
project execution and the co rresponding sections of the document. The Standards is not 
intended as a set of mandatory requirements for a project, but instead as guidance on how 
to develop the project requirements. It is understood that requirements will vary for project 
to project and practitioners may use this Standards as a foundation to further develop their 
BIM Project Execution Plan.
