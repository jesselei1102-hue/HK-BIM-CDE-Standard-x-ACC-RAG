2.2. CDE hosted in the cloud environment 
CDE can be hosted in the cloud or on-premises environment. In practice, most CDE products are 
hosted in the cloud environment. There are mainly four types of cloud model for CDE as shown in Fig. 
8. In general, public cloud for CDE is more economical than others as the IT infrastructure of the 
public cloud is provided and maintained by external Cloud Service Provider. 
A discussi on of the selection of cloud service provider is outside the scope of this Guide. It is 
suggested that companies  should obtain professional advice from IT -related professionals on this 
area.
