2.13 Labels, Tags and Dimensioning 
Users can annotate Civil 3D objects with labels that update dynamically when the drawing 
objects change. Labels can contain single or multiple lines of text, blocks, ticks, lines, and 
direction arrows. Many label styles have two modes: label and tag. Tags are used to identify 
labelled objects within a table. Please see the link below for more information.
