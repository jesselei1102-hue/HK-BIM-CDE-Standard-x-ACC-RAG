# Appendix J — Curtain Wall / Window Wall / Cladding Fast Track

<!-- page 35 -->
Appendix J 
(PNAP ADM-19) 
 
 
Fast Track Processing for Repair Works of Curtain Wall, 
Window Wall1 and Cladding 
 
 
 To enable BD to process the application for fast-track approval and consent 
within 30 days2, the following shall be submitted concurrently: 
 
 (a)  A supervision plan for the curtain wall, window wall or cladding works, 
where applicable, prepared by the AP , RSE and Registered General 
Building Contractor (RGBC) in accordance  with the Technical 
Memorandum for Supervision Plans and the Code of Practice for Site 
Supervision; 
 
 (b)  If tempered glass is to be installed, the RSE shall submit the following 
documents as stipulated in PNAP APP-37- 
 
  (i) quality supervision plan prepared by the RSE and 
RGBC for the quality supervision of manufacturer’s 
heat soak process, and 
 
  (ii) a copy of Quality Assurance Scheme issued by the 
manufacturer; 
 
 (c)   If the thermal transmittance characteristics of the replaced external wall 
materials regarding insulation is inferior to  the approved one, 
provisional Overall Thermal Transfer Value report under PNAP APP -
67 and, where applicable, provisional energy efficiency report under 
PNAP APP-156
3; and 
 
 (d) If the repair works involve a change/upgrading of the approved 
materials, full justifications of their structural adequacy, non -
combustibility, fire resisting properties in compliance with the relevant 
current Building Regulations, design standards and codes of practice. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
(2/2021) 
                                                           
1  Window wall, including glass wall and glass shop front, refers to window and wall formed by 
structural glass elements spanning between floors. 
2  Paragraph 31 of PNAP ADM-19 refers. 
3  Applicable to developments previously approved subject to the Building (Energy Efficiency) 
Regulation or PNAP APP-156.
